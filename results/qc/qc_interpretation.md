# QC Interpretation

We evaluated ATAC-seq quality for human and mouse adrenal gland and uterus datasets using TSS enrichment, pooled FRiP, and reproducible peak counts.

## Adrenal gland
Human adrenal showed very strong TSS enrichment (19.19 and 20.39) and high pooled FRiP (0.565), with 150,470 reproducible peaks. Mouse adrenal also showed strong TSS enrichment (18.37 and 19.00), acceptable pooled FRiP (0.287), and 47,792 reproducible peaks.

## Uterus
Human uterus showed mixed quality, with one replicate having TSS enrichment of 13.88 and the other only 6.78, along with lower pooled FRiP (0.184). Mouse uterus showed low TSS enrichment (5.70 and 6.24), low pooled FRiP (0.153), and 42,412 reproducible peaks.

## Conclusion
Adrenal gland has substantially better and more consistent QC metrics in both species than uterus. We therefore selected adrenal gland for downstream cross-species analysis.
